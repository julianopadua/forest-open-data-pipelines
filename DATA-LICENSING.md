Dataset files remain governed by the original source provider's terms, license, and attribution requirements. Users are responsible for reviewing those terms before reusing or redistributing any dataset.

## Source provenance

The project preserves official source provenance. Dataset manifests and catalog metadata use official source URLs for discovery, reproducibility, and attribution.
